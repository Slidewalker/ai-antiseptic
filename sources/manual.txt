
### How to turn this into `sources/manual.pdf` in 5 seconds

**Option 1 – Online (easiest)**
1. Go to https://md-to-pdf.fly.dev
2. Paste the Markdown above
3. Click “Convert” → download → rename to `manual.pdf`
4. Drop it into your `sources/` folder

**Option 2 – Command line (if you have pandoc)**
```bash
pandoc manual.md -o sources/manual.pdf --pdf-engine=weasyprint
# or with a nice template:
pandoc manual.md -o sources/manual.pdf --template=eisvogel --listings
